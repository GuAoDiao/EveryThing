缩放值:
MoveForceScale = 10.f;
SpeedScale = 10.f;
JumpForceScale = 75.f;

实际值:
ActualMoveForce = Quality * QualityScale * MoveForceScale;
ActualJumpForce = Agility * Quality * QualityScale * JumpForceScale;
ActualSpeed = Agility * SpeedScale;

旋转移动:

SpeedForceBase = ActualMoveForce * ActualSpeed;
JumpForceBase = ActualJumpForce;

跳跃移动:


JumpForwardForce = ActualMoveForce * ActualSpeed * 1.4f;
JumpHeightForce = ActualJumpForce ;
AdjustLocationForce = ActualMoveForce * ActualSpeed * 0.7f;
